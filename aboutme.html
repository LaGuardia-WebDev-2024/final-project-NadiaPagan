About Me

My name is Nadia Pagan. I am currently a student at Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School of Music & Art and Performing Arts and will be graduating in June of 2025. I am hardworking and work well in groups and alone. I am a creative thinker and incorporate creative aspects into whatever I'm working on. I have always enjoyed artistic things such as ceramics, drawing, design and sewing. I also have experience working in professional environments including Off-Broadway theater and have a lot of experience working with kids between the ages of 2-11.

In the technical theater program at laguardia i focas on technican theater where i have 4 years of experince in sound, lighting ,sceanery and costumes. i have worked on both school and profesional shows.
